## Full description
WearPod is a standalone podcast player built for round Wear OS watches.

It is designed for people who want to listen directly on the watch instead of relying on a phone companion app for everyday playback. Import subscriptions on your phone, keep listening on your watch, and take episodes offline before you head out.

What WearPod offers:
- Standalone podcast playback on Wear OS
- Phone-assisted subscription import with QR code flow
- Phone-assisted OPML export for backup and migration
- Offline downloads, download queue, and storage cleanup tools
- Playback controls built for the watch: play, pause, previous, next, speed, sleep timer, and output switching
- Watch-first layouts tuned for round displays
- English and Simplified Chinese support

WearPod focuses on a simple listening workflow:
1. Import feeds from your phone
2. Browse subscriptions and episodes on the watch
3. Download episodes for offline listening
4. Control playback directly from the watch

The app is intentionally lightweight:
- No phone companion app required
- No recommendation feed or social layer
- No account setup for basic public RSS listening

WearPod is a good fit if you want:
- A clean podcast player built specifically for Wear OS
- Direct RSS-based listening instead of a locked platform catalog
- Offline listening during walks, workouts, commutes, or travel

WearPod is still evolving. Upcoming work focuses on stronger long-term usability, including better support for private feeds, search, and richer episode details.
