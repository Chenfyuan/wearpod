# Content Rating Draft

This is a practical draft for the Play content rating questionnaire, based on the current WearPod feature set.

## Expected rating direction
- Likely suitable for a broad general audience
- No in-app violence, gambling, or sexual content produced by the app itself
- No user-to-user chat or social posting features

## Current product characteristics
- Streams and downloads podcast feeds selected by the user
- Does not include a recommendation feed curated by the app
- Does not provide social interaction or user-generated posting
- Does not include paid betting, dating, or gambling features
- Does not include location-based social discovery

## Review points before submitting
- Podcasts themselves can contain mature language or themes depending on user subscriptions
- Because content comes from external RSS feeds, answer the questionnaire carefully and avoid overstating moderation guarantees
- If you later add discovery, comments, or private community features, revisit the content rating

## Suggested internal note
Treat the app as a podcast utility / player, not as a curated social or news platform, when answering the questionnaire.
